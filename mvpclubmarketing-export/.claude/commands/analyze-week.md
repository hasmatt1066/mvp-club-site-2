# Analyze Week

Review this week's marketing performance and get recommendations.

## Context

This command analyzes your marketing metrics and content performance to identify what's working, what's not, and what to do next week.

## Instructions

1. **Gather metrics**: Ask the user to provide or point to:
   - Weekly metrics Google Sheet (or paste key numbers)
   - Post performance log
   - Any notable events (viral post, lots of DMs, etc.)

2. **If no data available**: Ask user to provide:
   - Total LinkedIn impressions this week
   - Average engagement rate
   - Follower growth
   - Top performing post (topic + metrics)
   - Worst performing post (topic + metrics)
   - Email metrics (if sent)
   - New community members
   - Discovery calls booked

3. **Analyze performance**:

### Top Performers

Identify the 2-3 best performing pieces:
- What was the topic?
- What format?
- What made it work?
- How can we do more like this?

### Underperformers

Identify the 2-3 worst performing pieces:
- What was the topic?
- What format?
- Why might it have flopped?
- Should we retry differently or abandon?

### Pattern Analysis

Look for patterns:
- **Topic patterns**: Which content pillars perform best?
- **Format patterns**: Stories vs. tactical vs. thesis?
- **Timing patterns**: Best days/times?
- **Voice patterns**: Which founder's content resonates most?
