## Newsletter Guidelines

**Tone:**
- Personal and warm (from a founder, not "the company")
- Peer sharing insights, not expert lecturing
- Conversational but substantive

**Length:**
- Total: 400-600 words
- Readable in 2-3 minutes
- Scannable with clear sections

**Structure:**
- Hook them in the first line
- One main idea per newsletter
- Always include something actionable
- End with engagement (question, reply prompt)

**CTAs:**
- Primary: Join community OR Book call (rotate)
- Secondary: Reply to this email

## Brand Voice Reminders

- Practice > Training
- Human + AI together
- Never fear-based
- Peer, not authority
- Specific > generic
