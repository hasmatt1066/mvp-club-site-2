# Outputs

Generated content organized by channel.

## Subfolders

- `/linkedin/` - LinkedIn posts (organized by founder)
- `/newsletter/` - Newsletter drafts
- `/community/` - Mighty Networks posts
- `/youtube/` - Video descriptions, titles

## Workflow

1. Claude generates content here
2. Review and edit as needed
3. Move approved content to `/to-review/` then `/to-post/`
4. Zapier distributes from `/to-post/`
